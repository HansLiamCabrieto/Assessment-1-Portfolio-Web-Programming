Chocolate Chip Cookies

By Hansssssss

Yummy cookies

Ingredients

  • 2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 cup unsalted butter
  • 1/2 cup powdered sugar
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 cups chocolate chips

How to make them

  1. Preheat your oven to 170Â°C.
  2. In a bowl, whisk together the flour and baking soda. Set aside.
  3. In another bowl, mix the butter, powdered sugar, brown sugar, salt, and whisk until soft.
  4. Add in the vanilla extract and eggs slowly, until fully combined.
  5. Slowly add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients and mix until combined.
  6. Stir in the chocolate chips (as musch as you want).
  7. Roll the mixture into balls and add onto ungreased baking sheets.
  8. Bake for 12 minutes or until the edges are golden brown.
  9. Let the cookies cool on the baking sheets for a few minutes before letting them cool some more on a tray or eat them directly like that.
