Overview

Genshin Impact is a 2020 action role-playing game developed by MiHoYo/HoYoverse. The game features an anime-style open world and an action-based battle system using elemental magic and character-switching. The game is free-to-play, with monetization through gacha game mechanics. The storyline follows the Traveler as they explore the world of Teyvat in search of their lost sibling, battling mysterious forces and uncovering secrets about Teyvat.
